## mobile-app-assessment

### "Packaged" Security Expertise

- **Codified a mobile application security assessment approach** aligned to OWASP MASVS v2.0 **to enable SEs who are *not* security SMEs to facilitate valuable, credible conversations with CISOs and application security teams** during enterprise sales cycles
- **Created an assessment toolkit** (including decompilation, credential scanning, obfuscation analysis, hook/swizzle detection) **to guide manual and AI-assisted workflows to progress opportunities and document post-remediation results**
- **Provided professional-grade reporting templates** with CVSS v3.1 scoring and OWASP MASVS v2.0 mapping, **enabling SEs to deliver security assessment reports** that meet enterprise governance and compliance expectations

### Assessment Efficiency

- **Designed an AI-assisted assessment workflow that reduced time to conduct evaluations** from 8-12 hours to 4-6 hours while maintaining OWASP MASVS coverage across 20 security controls
- **Enabled SEs to focus on higher-value analysis and stakeholder communication** by automating initial reconnaissance and pattern-based vulnerability discovery, 
- **Enhanced the assessment framework to include a mostly-automated comparative analysis** to track remediation effectiveness across app versions, supporting ongoing customer engagement beyond initial assessment

### Trust-Building & Technical Consulting

- Established a repeatable assessment process and framework to both **demonstrate methodology rigor (not just findings) and explain how findings were identified and scored**
- Developed a three-phase remediation roadmap approach (emergency / short-term / long-term) to **align recommendations with business timelines to make findings actionable rather than overwhelming**
- **Provided multi-audience deliverables for stakeholder communication**: detailed technical reports for engineering teams, executive summaries for leadership and email templates for stakeholder communication

## Cross-Project Themes

Regarding SE approach and philosophy:

### Engineering Excellence in Sales

- **Consistently invest in building reusable assets that compound in value** — each framework served multiple prospect engagements and reduced marginal effort over time
- **Apply software engineering best practices to SE deliverables** (version control, CI/CD, containerization, documentation) to showcase technical credibility during evaluations
- **Balance technical depth with business applicability** — every framework includes clear outcome narratives, architecture documentation and getting-started guides alongside the code

### Strategic Technical Leadership

- **Identify gaps in the SE toolkit** (demo consistency, security evaluation capability) and **proactively build frameworks to address them** to improve team-wide effectiveness
- **Design frameworks with extensibility in mind** — modular architecture enables other SEs to add new scenarios without learning everything about the implementation
- **Contribute to community knowledge** by open-sourcing reusable frameworks, strengthening the broader ecosystem and demonstrating thought leadership

### Business Impact Orientation

- **Approach every technical investment through a sales impact lens**: Will this shorten deal cycles? Enable new conversations? Improve win rates in competitive scenarios?
- **Quantify SE impact in business terms**: POC cycle time reduction, demo setup efficiency, assessment throughput, multi-persona engagement applicability
- **Build bridges to desired business outcomes from technical capabilities** — translating framework features into prospect-facing value propositions
